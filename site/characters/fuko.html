Lore

Fuko Una is a sentient AI robot cat living in a artificial world filled with oblivious Npc's, where nothing ever happens. No matter how much the world tries to replicate "real life events", it all feels fake.

Fuko doesn't know if any of his memories are true. She tried many times to make friends, but no one can understand their worldview. It has tried to "escape", but couldn't due to the programming. Every time they try, they just end up at the same place.

Out of despair and boredom, it has turned to self endangering behavior that can sometimes end up in serious injuries, knowing that they cannot die.
