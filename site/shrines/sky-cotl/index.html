What is Sky?

Sky: Children of the light (COTL) is a social MMO from creators of Journey. It's a cozy, open-world game, where you explore the kingdom of Sky, collect light and candles, solve puzzles, help spirits and make friends.

Welsome home, child.
Regain your child-like sense of wonder in this social adventure game.

This game is very cozy and has beautiful visuals.
