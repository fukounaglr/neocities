What is Sky?

Sky: Children of the light (COTL) is a social MMO from creators of Journey. It's a cozy, open-world game, where you explore the kingdom of Sky, collect light and candles, solve puzzles, help spirits and make friends.

Welsome home, child.
Regain your child-like sense of wonder in this social adventure game.

Your main item is your wing (cape) - it gives you the ability to fly. You level up you wing by finding Winged Light and getting Wing Buffs from spirits. The more level your wing is, the more flaps you can make. Stars on the cape indicate your level. For example, my cape on this image has 8 stars, which means my cape is lvl 8!
